Project LEAD

About Us

Project LEAD (Learn Everything Applying DIY) is an Illinois non-profit focusing on encouraging interest in the STEM (Science Techonlogy Engineering and Math) fields.

Project LEAD is working on sharing some of the most interesting aspects of the STEM felds with the world through social media, and at no cost to the consumer.

 

Project LEAD has also taken part in a number of Maker Faires, and will be sponsoring a Hackathon in Chicago.

Project LEAD is run by an amazing group of people. Info about each can be found below!
