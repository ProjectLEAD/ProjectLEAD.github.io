Project LEAD

Project LEAD is an Illinois Based Non-Profit Encouraging and fostering interest in the STEM (Sci, Tech, Engi, and Math) fields through online content AND real-world interaction!

LEAD stands for Learn Everything Applying DIY, because learning by doing is the best, and that hands-on experience gives a personal tie to the project that is created.

Keep up-to-date with Project LEAD by checking back periodically!

Our Mission:

Project LEAD's goal is to increase awareness and interest in STEM fields. We have gone about this in a variety of ways, but have especially focused on the digital medium. We have published well over 50 videos and counting with more being added every week! We have reached people in 168 (of the 196) countries in the world, even some in countries where they aren't supposed to have YouTube.

 

Outside of our digital activities we also do local events like Maker Faires, and have presented to school classes many times discussing anything from high-altitude balloons to 3D Printing.
