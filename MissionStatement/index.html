Mission Statement

Project LEAD is and Illinois based non-profit that is working to further encourage and inspire students to investigate STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math). Project LEAD utilizes a unique combo of digital, and real-world interactions. Project LEAD's team members have contributed to numerous events and have reached out to schools in multiple states.  Our members are particularly fond of Maker Faires and have been makers a 2 in 2013 (and hopefully more in 2014!). Project LEADs main goal is to spread STEM through online means. Facebook and YouTube drive most of Project LEAD's traffic, and Project LEAD has reached nearly every country that has access to YouTube, and even some that aren't supposed to (we can't say we aren't happy about that).
